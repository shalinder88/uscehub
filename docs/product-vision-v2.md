# Phase 3 Product Vision v2 — The Human-Centered Approach
## March 2026

## The Core Insight

The J-1/H-1B physician immigration experience is not an information problem.
It's an anxiety problem.

Every physician going through this describes the same thing: constant low-grade
dread punctuated by moments of panic. They don't need more articles about
Conrad 30. They need to feel like they know where they stand.

## What We Have (Content Layer — DONE)
- 50 state waiver guides with application windows
- Interactive US map with slot tracking
- 6 waiver pathways compared with step-by-step processes
- H-1B guide, green card pathways, contract checklist
- Salary benchmarks, malpractice guide, state financial comparison
- Timeline calculator, HPSA lookup, visa journey flowchart
- Licensing guide, job sources, interview prep
- 10 blog posts for SEO

This is the foundation. It's what brings people to the site via Google.
It's our SEO moat. It's done.

## What We Need (Engagement Layer — BUILD NEXT)

### 1. Visa Bulletin Tracker (Monthly Check)
- Show EB-2 and EB-3 movement for India, China, ROW
- Input: "I filed I-140 on [date], born in [country]"
- Output: estimated wait time with historical trend chart
- Monthly email alert when bulletin moves
- THIS is the tool that gets checked monthly for years

### 2. Policy Alert Feed (Weekly Check)
- Curated immigration news that affects physicians specifically
- $100K H-1B fee updates
- Conrad 30 reauth status
- USCIS processing time changes
- Executive order impacts
- H-4 EAD rule changes
- Simple, clean feed — not a firehose

### 3. Employer Job Posting System (MONETIZATION)
- Hospitals post waiver-eligible positions: $200-500/listing
- They already pay recruiters $15K-25K per placement
- We have the audience they need
- This is the primary business model

### 4. Attorney Directory (MONETIZATION)
- Immigration attorneys specializing in physician waivers
- Attorneys pay $500-2K/month for verified listing
- Physicians get vetted recommendations
- Include reviews from physicians who used them

## What We Should NOT Build Yet
- Mobile app (website works fine on mobile)
- Full community forum (too early, need critical mass)
- AI chatbot (not needed yet)
- Complex calculators nobody uses twice
- Account system / dashboard (too early for user accounts)

## The Growth Flywheel
Content (SEO) → Physicians find us → They bookmark for tracker/alerts →
They share with colleagues → More physicians → Employers want to post →
Revenue funds more content → Repeat

## Monetization Priority
1. Employer job postings ($200-500/listing) — direct revenue
2. Attorney directory sponsorships ($500-2K/month) — recurring
3. Affiliate revenue (WES 10%, Lecturio 15%, CompHealth $1K) — passive
4. Premium alerts/tracking ($10-20/month) — later, once trust is built

## The Emotional Truth
A physician from India, 2 years into their waiver in rural Ohio, opens
this site and sees:
- Their state's Conrad slots (tracker)
- The latest visa bulletin with their estimated timeline
- A policy alert that their H-4 spouse can now apply for EAD
- A new waiver job in a city their spouse actually wants to live in

That's not a website. That's a lifeline.
