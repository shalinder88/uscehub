Learn Medicine in an Unrivaled Ecosystem

Step into a 240-year legacy of global impact.

Situated in the world’s epicenter of health care, biomedical research, and life sciences, Harvard Medical School serves as a magnet for the brightest and most passionate clinicians, researchers, students, and changemakers in medicine.

12,000+

faculty

Twice the number of full-time faculty as any other medical school in the U.S., offering a mentor for every project you can imagine.

15

affiliate hospitals

An expansive network joining forces in science, health care, and community.

200+

elective courses

A rich selection of clinical and nonclinical experiences to support your path.

72%

of students receive financial aid

One of the most generous medical school aid programs in the country.
