About Denver Health

Denver Health is a comprehensive, integrated health system dedicated to providing community members with access to high-quality, equitable health care, regardless of their ability to pay. We extend that care across Denver through paramedics, public health programs, school-based health centers, and more. We also educate the next generation of health care professionals and conduct research to continuously improve the care we deliver.
