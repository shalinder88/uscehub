Visiting Student Program

Applicant Eligibility

Visiting students are eligible for electives at CUIMC if they meet the following criteria:

  1. Currently enrolled in their final year of medical school at one of the following:
    • Domestic: LCME-accredited school in the US/Canada, Osteopathic school
    • International: International Exchange Affiliation (must be pre-approved by home school)
  2. Completed at least 4-6 core clerkships in Internal Medicine, Surgery, and 2 other specialties (including the elective specialty in which they are applying, where available).

Elective Application Instructions

Eligible domestic visiting students must use VSLO to submit their applications. Eligible international visiting students should contact their school for approval and instructions on how to apply (also see: International Exchange Affiliation).

  • The list of VSLO application requirements can be viewed here.
  • To prevent delays in the onboarding process, please be prepared to submit the required information from the Student Health office once accepted.
  • Every effort will be made for decision notifications to be made 6-8 weeks prior to the start of the desired elective month(s).
Note that some electives have their own application and decision timelines, including:
