Watch/Listen To RUMC Media Richmond Health Network Make An Appointment Read Patient Testimonials… AWARD-WINNING CARE FROM STATEN ISLAND&#8217;S LEADING HOSPITAL: RICHMOND UNIVERSITY MEDICAL CENTER Richmond University Medical Center (RUMC) is an award‑winning, 440+‑bed teaching hospital serving Staten Island and the Greater New York City area. As a full‑service healthcare facility, RUMC delivers comprehensive inpatient and outpatient care, including emergency and trauma services, surgery, pediatrics, geriatrics, and behavioral health. Beyond the hospital campus, RUMC extends care through the Richmond Health Network, a community‑based system of more than 200 providers across 20+ locations throughout Staten Island, with additional offices in Brooklyn and Manhattan. Together, RUMC and the Richmond Health Network form one of Staten Island’s most integrated, community‑focused health systems—providing high‑quality, coordinated care that keeps patients close to home and connected to trusted medical expertise.
