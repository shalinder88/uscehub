Higher-strength allergy relief is available

Don't let seasonal allergies get in the way of enjoying the warmer weather. Find relief from symptoms like itchy eyes, runny nose, sore throat and recurring headaches. Henry Ford Primary Care doctors can provide higher-strength treatments than over-the-counter options.

Get answers this rash season

Rashes can appear on the skin for a variety of reasons. Did you know that spring is peak season for reactions from Poison Ivy? Whether you brush up against something outdoors or you have a chronic condition like eczema dn rosacea that can worsen as temperatures rise, our Henry Ford Dermatologists will find the most effective treatment that works for you.

Find a Dermatologist

Get relief from a sports-related injury

Don’t let an injury keep you on the sidelines this spring. If you have a sports-related injury or condition, Henry Ford Sports Medicine can help you get back in the game.

We Are Henry
Whether it's the challenge of a marathon or the sprint to save lives with specialized care, these stories highlight what it means to be Henry.

By all accounts, Donna Pompa of Northville, has been the picture of health. An intensive care nurse for 15 years before devoting herself to raising her daughter, Donna has led an active healthy lifestyle including exercising and gardening. At age 40, Donna obtained her first mammogram at Henry Ford West Bloomfield Hospital and the screening revealed potentially alarming news.
Read Donna's story

After discussing her options with her Henry Ford Health bariatric surgery team, and with the support of her family and pastor, Crystal Crump chose to have a Roux-en-Y gastric bypass, performed by bariatric surgeon Bilal Kharbutli, M.D., at Henry Ford Wyandotte Hospital.
Read Crystal's Story

The Henry Ford Health Cardiac Rehabilitation program is for people who have left the hospital after a heart attack, heart procedure or heart failure. Participation in a cardiac rehabilitation program after a qualifying event is supported by the strongest possible level of evidence. Benefits include significant reductions in cardiovascular complications as well as improvements in quality of life.

Recognized for clinical excellence

Henry Ford Health is one of the nation's leading academic medical centers, partnering with more than 2 million people on their health journey in communities across Michigan and around the world. We provide a full suite of health services including primary and preventative care, complex and specialty care, health insurance, home health and virtual care, and retail including pharmacy and eye care.
