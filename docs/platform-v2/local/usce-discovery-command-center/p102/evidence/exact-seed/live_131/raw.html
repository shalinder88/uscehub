Patient care and serving our community are top priorities at UCI Health. Over the previous 12 months, the Pediatric ICU and Pediatric floor at UCI Health – Fountain Valley averaged fewer than 3 patients daily. Given the significant demand for adult patient care, these two units are being transitioned to better align with community needs and will become adult beds effective April 21, 2026. Newborn services, including maternity, postpartum and NICU care, will all continue at Fountain Valley.
