Medical Student Rotations & Observerships

Medical Student Rotations & Observerships

Medical student rotations and observerships at Houston Methodist give residents the proper amount of time and responsibility needed to achieve and surpass their professional goals.

Residents learn in a variety of settings, such as working with inpatient teams and one-on-one with subspecialists. Our rotations and observerships provide in-depth, hands-on experience in a range of fields.

Rotations

Our goal is to provide medical students with an environment that is conducive to excellence in education and training in all areas of medicine. Our faculty strives to develop leaders in providing quality patient care and community service as we encourage professional and personal health, growth and development. 

Observerships

If you are interested in observing a Houston Methodist physician, please submit your inquiry to HMObserver@houstonmethodist.org

 

If you are an international physician, resident or medical student who is seeking to observe a clinical practice, please contact the Global Education Office.
