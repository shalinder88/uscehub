VSLO - Visiting Student Learning Opportunities

Visit sunny Southern California in your fourth year.

UCLA has an expansive clinical footprint spanning the greater Los Angeles area, giving you a wide spectrum of patient care learning opportunities.  As a visiting student, you’ll meet Angelenos, folks from around the globe, from all walks of life, and experience the diversity of the city while you are at it.

VSLO FAQ

How do I apply to the VSLO program?

The David Geffen School of Medicine at UCLA is uses the AAMC's Visiting Student Learning Opportunities (VSLO) program, to receive visiting student applications. To apply to our institution, please complete a VSLO application for your preferred electives and dates. 

VSLO Application

Who is eligible to apply?

Students in their fourth year at a U.S. medical school. VSLO applicants must be in good academic standing and actively progressing toward a Doctor of Medicine degree at an affiliated LCME-accredited institution, or a Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine degree at an affiliated COCA-accredited AACOM Member Institution. Learn more on the US Students VLSO page.

U.S. Student Information 

Are international students eligible to apply?

Due to limited course availability, our international VSLO elective program is now only open to pre-selected students from schools with which we have established reciprocal exchange agreements. Thank you for your understanding.
