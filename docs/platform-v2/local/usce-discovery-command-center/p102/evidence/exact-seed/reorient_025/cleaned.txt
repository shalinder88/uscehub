Scheduling rotations: Students from Affiliated Medical Schools: Core clerkships and sub-internships must be scheduled through the medical school. Electives may be scheduled through the medical school or by contacting the Hospital/Department Student Coordinator directly. See Site Specific Information below. Students from Non-Affiliated Medical School: Students from non-affiliated schools are only eligible for 3rd or 4th year elective rotations at the sites listed above. Assignment is based on availability. Electives may be scheduled by contacting the Hospital/Department Student Coordinator directly. See Site Specific Information below. Forms/requirements: Once a rotation is approved and scheduled, onboarding tasks will be initiated. All onboarding tasks must be completed a minimum of FOUR (4) weeks prior to the scheduled rotation start date. ROTATIONS MAY BE CANCELLED IF ALL ONBOARDING TASKS ARE NOT COMPLETED BY THE ESTABLISHED DEADLINE. View additional information and online forms now.
