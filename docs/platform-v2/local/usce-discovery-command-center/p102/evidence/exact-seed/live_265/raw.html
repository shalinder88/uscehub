Comprehensive Services

As the trusted hospital in Queens, New York, we are dedicated to providing every clinical service necessary to address your diverse health needs across a wide range of specialized medical fields. Here are some of our clinical services:

Emergency Services

Our Emergency Department provides 24/7 care for all emergencies. We treat approximately 44,000 patients annually. Our team is trained to care for adult and pediatric patients in every critical situation.

Cardiology

The Cardiology Division of our hospital in Queens, New York, provides state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic services. Our team utilizes the latest technology to treat heart-related issues in a supportive environment.

Read More

Women’s Health

The women’s health department at Flushing Hospital in Queens, New York, offers a full spectrum of services. We understand the unique needs of women and are dedicated to providing care during pregnancy, childbirth, and all stages of life.

Read More

Robotic Surgery

Flushing Hospital features cutting-edge robotic surgery technology, enhancing precision in procedures. Our expert surgeons use the da Vinci Surgical System, offering patients quicker recoveries, reduced pain, and improved surgical outcomes.

Read More

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics Department offers comprehensive care for children, from newborns to young adults. With over 12,000 pediatric visits yearly, our Queens, New York hospital provides specialized services for young patients in a child-friendly environment.

Bariatric Surgery

The Bariatric Surgery Center at FHMC helps individuals struggling with obesity. Our program includes surgical options and tailored support to guide patients toward healthier lifestyles and lasting weight loss results.

Read More

Mental Health Services

We offer an array of services for individuals facing challenges with substance use or mental health disorders. Our expert team provides inpatient and outpatient support, focusing on compassionate care to promote psychological well-being in the community.

Read More

Dental Services

Our Dental Department offers comprehensive care for patients of all ages. With modern facilities and a dedicated team, our Queens, New York hospital focuses on preventative care and treatments for complex dental needs.

Read More

Ambulatory Care

Our newly renovated Ambulatory Care Center features a range of specialty services. Open Monday to Friday, we focus on convenience and comfort, providing high-quality outpatient services to meet the needs of our diverse community.

Welcome
To Flushing Hospital

Since 1884, Flushing Hospital has proudly served the culturally rich Queens, New York Community. As the first hospital in the borough, we are dedicated to providing compassionate care in a comfortable environment.

We aim to deliver the highest quality, most cost-effective service to our patients and community. Located conveniently near LaGuardia Airport, Flushing Hospital can meet the needs of patients at every stage of life.

Whether you need emergency care, specialized treatment, or routine health services, we are here for you. Explore our wide range of services and learn why our hospital in Queens, New York, is the trusted healthcare provider you can count on.
