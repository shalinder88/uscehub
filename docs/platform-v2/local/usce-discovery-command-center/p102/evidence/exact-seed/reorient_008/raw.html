Growing an International Community of Health Care Innovators

As part of UPMC’s mission to provide outstanding patient care and shape tomorrow’s health system, our clinical collaborations and training programs provide valuable medical knowledge and operational expertise to our partners and patients around the world.

  • UPMC’s long-standing affiliation with the University of Pittsburgh, a leader in medical research, ensures that we continually offer the most up-to-date and innovative clinical insights available.
  • The UPMC International Training Center offers a series of hands-on observership and management training programs at our international and domestic UPMC facilities.
  • UPMC's program for traveling patients connects physicians and patients around the world with UPMC services both internationally and in the U.S.
  • The UPMC Concussion Network is the international expansion of our UPMC concussion program which aims to create a standardized approach to consensus-driven and evidence-based treatment pathways.
