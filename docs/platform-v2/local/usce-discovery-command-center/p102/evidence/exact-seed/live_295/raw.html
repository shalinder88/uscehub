Emergency Department & Urgent Care Locations

We are here for life’s emergencies. If you need help right away, call 911.

Our conveniently located emergency department and urgent care locations help you access care when and where you need it.

Emergency departments treat life-threatening symptoms. Urgent care is for problems that can’t wait but aren’t life threatening. Check current wait times and locations before you go. 

Go to the Emergency Room for Serious Concerns:

  • Chest pain, trouble breathing, or signs of stroke
  • Severe injuries such as major fractures, head trauma, or uncontrolled bleeding
  • Sudden, severe pain or symptoms that threaten life or health

See Emergency Department Locations and Current Wait Times

Go to Urgent Care for Less Threatening Problems:

  • Minor injuries like small cuts, sprains, or mild fractures
  • Common illnesses like colds, flu, ear infections, or sore throats
  • Urgent but non-life-threatening issues

See Urgent Care Locations and Current Wait Times

Explore Our Services

Primary Care

Staying well means caring for your health every day. Our primary care providers offer routine screenings, disease management and prevention, and healthy habit suggestions to keep you at your best.

Women's Health

We care for women of all ages in all stages. We offer OB/GYN checkups, preventive health screenings, midwife care, and more. Our specialties include breast and reproductive health. We also specialize in gynecological cancer treatment.

Pediatrics

We provide a long list of pediatric services in collaboration with Primary Children’s Hospital. Our providers located throughout the Mountain West care for your child from infancy to adulthood.

Heart Health

Our Cardiovascular Center at University of Utah Hospital includes heart care, heart surgery, and treatment for all types of heart disease.
