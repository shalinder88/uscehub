Browse Programs University Hospitals Provides In-depth Clinical Training for Enriching Medical Careers As one of the nation&rsquo;s top health systems, University Hospitals is committed to teaching excellence, in a supportive and collegial atmosphere, as we train the next generation of healthcare leaders. We are an academic hub, with world-renowned faculty providing rigorous and in-depth training, including access to nationally acclaimed research programs. With strong leadership across all clinical specialties, we teach leading-edge techniques and innovative clinical practices to prepare our residents and fellows for board eligibility in their specialties and enriching medical careers. All residents and fellows participate in experiential learning in leadership, in collaboration with the UH Leadership Institute, as well as improving the value of care for individuals and populations, in collaboration with our UH Quality Institute . Residents are peer-selected to represent programs at our GME Resident Forum, which fosters bidirectional communication with system leaders, including our Innovation and Clinical Transformation teams. UH Programs Meet or Exceed ACGME Requirements Graduate medical education (GME) leadership at University Hospitals ensures that all programs meet or exceed the institutional and program requirements declared by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME). Our GME staff is critical to this comprehensive learning experience, as they assist our residents and fellows in identifying the wide range of services available to them. We are committed to a positive learning experience, supporting the trainees, faculty and staff. Designated Institutional Official: Elizabeth Harcher Letter from the DIO Why Train at UH?
