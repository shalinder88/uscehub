Graduate Medical Education

Advancing Medical Excellence

Preparing future physician leaders through our nationally recognized programs.

Explore our programsWelcome message

What We Do

UC Davis Health faculty provide residents and fellows with mentorship in a supportive and challenging educational environment in state-of-the-art facilities, where individuals participate in delivering safe, ethical, and leading-edge care.

850+ full-time residents and fellows in our clinical training programs

75 ACGME-accredited training programs (Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education)

110+ Training Programs, both ACGME and non-ACGME residency and fellowship programs

Live and Work in a Vibrant Community

Why Choose UC Davis School of Medicine?

As one of the nation’s leading medical schools and research institutions – we rank in the top 10 for family medicine, primary care and diversity – our faculty and staff are instrumental in preparing students and trainees to live a life of impact.

Living in Sacramento

Sacramento and its surrounding region is a uniquely situated and dynamic location offering an abundance of local farmer’s markets, nationally acclaimed restaurants that offer farm-to-fork cuisine, a variety of outdoor recreational sports, family-friendly events, art and culture.
