From Homeless to Hope: Joe’s Journey to Recovery | Hartford Hospital Community Health

For decades, Joe Ferone lived on the streets of Hartford, battling addiction that began when he was just a child. What started at age nine quickly spiraled into a life defined by survival, hardship, and the constant grip of substance use. But Joe’s story didn’t end there. In a moment of clarity, he walked into Hartford Hospital and asked for help – changing the course of his life forever. Through the dedicated, compassionate care of Hartford Hospital Community Health and addiction specialist Dr. Jason Wang, Joe found found support, dignity, and a path forward. Today, Joe is living proof that recovery is possible – with the right care, commitment, and community behind you.

Bringing Healthcare Beyond Hospital Walls | Hartford HealthCare Community Health

Health isn’t shaped by biology alone – it’s influenced by access, environment, and support. Through our Community Health programs, Hartford HealthCare shows up in neighborhoods across Hartford, delivering care beyond hospital walls to help close gaps, prevent illness, and make healthcare more accessible for all.
