The largest healthcare provider in west central Pennsylvania, Conemaugh's team of providers and staff serve over a half-million patients each year across its network of four hospitals, three outpatient centers and the Conemaugh Physician Group. As the region’s leading employer, Conemaugh strives to create a diverse working environment where employees, providers, residents, and students feel valued, recognized, and appreciated, and are empowered to help grow the next generation of talent to serve patients locally.
