Education Graduate Medical Education Back to Education Graduate Medical Education Montefiore Einstein is one of the nation’s preeminent centers for graduate medical education, drawing top talent globally and producing industry leaders committed to advancing human health. Residency & Fellowship Programs Located in the heart of New York City, Montefiore Einstein provides a unique opportunity for hands-on training in one of the most urban areas in the country—one where the population is global, the disease burden is high, and the need is great. We stand among the top 10 largest medical and surgical training programs in the country, providing postgraduate clinical training annually to over 1,500 residents and fellows across 100 accredited programs. Our mission to improve access and quality of care draws residents and fellows from top medical, dental and physician assistant schools from around the world. We develop and expand programs designed to advance teamwork, communication and decision-making, and our progressive focus on comprehensive care provides an ideal training ground for our residents and fellows. Explore Graduate Medical Education Resources Shaping the Future of Pediatric Medicine Children’s Hospital at Montefiore Einstein (CHAM) prioritizes creating lifelong learners in pediatric medicine. Our renowned faculty and comprehensive programs promote quality improvement, ethics, humanism and a comprehensive approach to clinical reasoning and diagnostics. With a central focus on serving our young community, our Department of Pediatrics offers extensive exposure through our residency and 19 specialized fellowship programs. Explore Pediatric Medicine GME Graduate Medical Education: Reimagining Residency Initiative Launched in 2019, the American Medical Association’s (AMA) Residency Initiative is a national effort to modernize the transition from medical school to residency, introduce relevant new curricula, and support resident well-being. Through a multi-million-dollar grant, Montefiore Einstein is advancing this work to better prepare physicians for today’s evolving healthcare environment.
