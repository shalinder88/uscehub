The Michigan State University Global Health Institute (GHI), part of the College of Osteopathic Medicine, serves as the focal point for global health information to all MSU colleges. A Global Mission for One Health The Global Health Institute looks to create international connections and research innovation through the concept of One Health: The protection and interdependence of human, animal, and environmental health through a multi-disciplinary approach.
