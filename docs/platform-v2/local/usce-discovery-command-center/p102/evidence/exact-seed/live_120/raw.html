Specialty Care

Your hospital for expert specialty care in the Bronx

We’re much more than an ER. You’ll find a full range of comprehensive specialty care services at SBH.

Trusted pediatric care for every child

Children deserve our very best. And so do you as their parent or caregiver. That’s why SBH is a Level 3 NCQA designated Medical Home. That means you don’t just get to see a doctor, you have 24/7 access to our pediatricians. Call after hours. Or on weekends. You’ll get a return call from our on-call pediatrician. We not only care for your children when they are sick, we help you keep them healthy with proper nutrition and exercise and, when necessary, helping you find services and resources in the community.
