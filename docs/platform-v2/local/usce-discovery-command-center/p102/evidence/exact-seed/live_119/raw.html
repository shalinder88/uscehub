Our program is dedicated to fostering educational opportunities for residents, as well as for prospective applicants from around the world and international medical graduates seeking experience in the United States healthcare system. Through our Observership Program, participants have the chance to engage with our department, participate in the daily routines of clinicians, learn about ongoing clinical practices, and observe the delivery of high-quality psychiatric care in a collaborative academic environment. This experience also offers a valuable insight into the culture of our residency program and the provision of patient care, interdisciplinary teamwork, and the principles that guide BronxCare’s approach to psychiatry. Observers are welcomed as part of the learning community, gaining perspectives on what it means to train and practice in a diverse and dynamic city.

Observership Programs Offered

  • Adult Inpatient Psychiatry
  • Addiction Psychiatry
  • Consult and Liaison (Psychosomatic Medicine)
  • Child & Adolescent Inpatient Psychiatry
  • Primary Care
  • Comprehensive Psychiatry Emergency Program (observe only)

Application Process

The Observership Program offers an orientation to the BronxCare Health System model of health care excellence and teamwork in an environment that offers a clinical focus and includes opportunities to participate in research and education. 

On the program application, please indicate your preference of rotations and dates available. Our rotations last a minimum of eight weeks and maximum of 12 weeks. If rotating at more than one site, you are required to rotate on the service a minimum of two weeks and maximum of four weeks. The minimum number of days each week on the service is three days per week, from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. The application fee is nonrefundable.

Observership Programs Offered (and Application Fees):

  • 1 month ($250)
  • 2 months ($400)
  • 3 months ($500)

Checks should be made payable to “BronxCare Health System Psychiatry Residency Training Program” and mailed to the attention of Ms. Noemi Edwards, Psychiatry Residency Training Program, 1276 Fulton Avenue, 5th Floor, Bronx, NY, 10456. You will be notified in writing either that your request is accepted or that we cannot accommodate. If we are unable to provide your requested rotation, you will be offered the next available rotation date. 

You may ask questions about the program or check the status of your application by sending an email to the Residency Training Program at Nedwards@bronxcare.org.
