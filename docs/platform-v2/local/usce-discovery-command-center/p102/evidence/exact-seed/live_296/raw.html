What Is Brooklyn USCE?

Brooklyn USCE was founded in 2015 by Dr. Ratesh Khillan, a board-certified Hematology-Oncologist. Brooklyn USCE aims to make rotations affordable to all International Medical Students and Graduates, to guide and help them on the path to residency in the United States. Every year applicants take the opportunity to enroll with us for On-site and Telemedicine rotations.

Clinical Rotations are a crucial part of an applicant’s USMLE journey and Residency application to become successful doctors in the United States. It is advisable for all applicants to earn performance-based Letters of Recommendation from Preceptors who are based in the US to strengthen their application.

Working and learning from US-based doctors gives them the opportunity to familiarize themselves with the US Healthcare system and prepare them for their US Medical Residency Program.
