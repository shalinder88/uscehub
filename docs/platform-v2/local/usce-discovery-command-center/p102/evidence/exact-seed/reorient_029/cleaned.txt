Teaching and learning good medicine requires observation and practice. This is present in our dedicated faculty who are passionate about the learning process and committed to medical education. They are excellent role models to the students and on many occasions, have become lifetime mentors. Practice through repetition is essential in medical training. The Applebaum Simulation Learning Institute is designed to change the way health care professionals learn and train. It provides students and residents with national leadership in the scientific application of modern simulation technologies to sharpen not only their procedural skills, but to also practice critical thinking skills by experiencing scenarios that they will encounter in practice. Corewell Health offers more than 50 elective rotations for fourth-year medical students representing many medical schools. These electives include sub-internships in family medicine, internal medicine, surgery and pediatrics. Application If you are a senior medical student, or entering your senior year of school, and are attending an AOA Commission on Osteopathic College Accreditation (COCA) institution, or the U.S. LCME accredited institution, please apply to our electives through VSAS (Visit Student Application Service) .
