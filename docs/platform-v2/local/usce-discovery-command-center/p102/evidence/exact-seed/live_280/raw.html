Paths to Possibilities

Come experience what's possible at UC Health.

UC Health is care centered around connecting research, clinical trials, care teams, and ongoing wellness — all to create a path to new possibilities. We invite you to take an experience of how, together, we are turning barriers into breakthroughs and reimagining recovery.

 

Patient-Centered Innovation

Research That's Improving Lives & Changing Medicine

Good enough is never enough when it comes to your health. That's why we never stop finding answers and improving lives. For 200+ years, UC Health has been transforming medical care for the Cincinnati community and beyond. As one of the nation’s top 25 public research universities, we are a powerhouse of innovation and expertise that brings together the region’s top clinicians and researchers, including the academic strength of the University of Cincinnati.

Our Purpose

To advance healing and reduce suffering.

Learn More

Our Mission

We are committed to advancing medicine and improving the health of all people–regardless of race, ethnicity, geography or ability to pay by fostering groundbreaking medical research and education, delivering outstanding primary and specialty care services, and building a diverse workforce.

Our Vision

To use the power of academic medicine to advance the science of discovery and transform the delivery of care.

Our Values

We will serve our patients, our community and one another by:

  • Putting people first
  • Showing respect
  • Acting with integrity
  • Embracing inclusion
  • Seeking discovery
  • Offering empathy
