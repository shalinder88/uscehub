Our Consulting & Advisory Services unites global partners to raise the bar of health care for all. Through our strategic relationships, such as with international governments and ministries of health, we provide the following advisory services:

Email us to learn more.

Direct clinical care

Telemedicine

  • Access to Northwell clinicians via telemedicine 24/7, 365 days a year
  • Provider-to-provider consults in all specialties

Medical tourism

  • Second opinion consults in all specialties
  • Complex cases and patient transfers with appointments in 24-48 hours

In-country care

  • On-site care in all specialties
  • Educational seminars for community outreach
Clinical education

Continuing medical education (CME) and graduate medical education (GME)

  • Customize in-person and virtual CME and GME programs
  • Provide virtual access to any Northwell lectures
  • Offer observership programs—two or four week rotations

Establish a bidirectional student exchange program with Zucker School of Medicine

  • Create physician shadowing program for attendings
  • Educate team members
  • Provide access to clinical education and professional development courses
    • Respiratory therapy, infection control, etc.
  • Offer first responder/Emergency medical technician/Paramedic training program

Research

  • Develop a clinical data exchange to provide access to robust clinical databases
Administrative & leadership education

Leadership development program

  • Provide a customized leadership training program focused on improving culture and achieving organizational goals

Team member programs

  • Center for Learning and Innovation (CLI)
  • Center for Traumatic Stress, Resilience and Recovery (CTSRR)
  • Culture of CARE training (COC)
Health system advisement

Hospital and facility management

  • Manage daily operations, streamline processes and procedures, staffing and financial management
  • Assess financial health of an organization and provide recommendations for future growth and improvement

Review quality outcomes and health care economics

  • Assess clinical quality, safety, outcomes and throughput for inpatient units
  • Assess emergency department wait times, access, flow and readmissions

Clinical program development

  • Provide expertise to support building new clinical programs/centers

Facility design and structure

  • Plan and develop clinical and non-clinical areas that match quality standards and optimize patient flow
