Comprehensive Services

At Jamaica Hospital Medical Center, we provide a wide array of clinical services to meet the health needs of our community. Here are some core services our hospital offers in Queens, New York City.

Ambulatory Care

Our Department of Ambulatory Care provides excellent outpatient services in a welcoming setting. We offer primary care and specialty services in Queens, New York. Jamaica Hospital’s knowledgeable staff ensures every visit is smooth and supportive.

Cardiology

JHMC is home to the Thomas Santucci Cardiovascular Interventional Suite. This advanced facility provides comprehensive heart care, including cardiac catheterization and arrhythmia services. We treat various cardiac conditions, ensuring patients receive effective treatments to manage their heart health.

Emergency Care

Jamaica Hospital’s Emergency Department operates 24/7, ready to treat over 117,000 patients a year. Our compassionate team includes board-certified emergency medicine specialists and nurses who provide the best care for every patient.

Pediatrics

The Pediatric Department of our hospital in Queens, New York, provides comprehensive care for children from infancy through adolescence. With specialized pediatric services, we ensure every child receives the highest level of care in a comforting environment.

Neurology

The JHMC Division of Neurology offers inpatient and outpatient services for a comprehensive spectrum of neurological disorders and conditions. Migraines, neuropathy, MS, vertigo, epilepsy, dementia, Parkinson’s disease, and more are treated by our New York City hospital’s multidisciplinary team.

Stroke Care

Our Comprehensive Stroke Center is the first and only one of its kind in Queens, and a top 5 stroke center in the state of New York, with 24/7 in-house coverage, expert neuro-interventionists and neuro-intensivists, and specialized equipment, as well as extensive coverage in both general neurosurgery and cerebrovascular neurosurgery.

Vascular Care

The Division of Vascular and Endovascular Surgery at JHMC treats circulatory system conditions like arterial disease, deep vein thrombosis, stroke, and aneurysms with holistic care and minimally invasive procedures. Our medical team employs long-term lifestyle plans for patients to identify risks and ensure vascular health in months and years to come.

Women’s Health

Our hospital offers comprehensive OB/GYN services in Queens, New York. From prenatal care through birthing to regular gynecological services, Jamaica Hospital is committed to the health and well-being of women at all stages of life.

Cancer Care

Jamaica Hospital’s affiliation with Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center helps to make our integrative oncology program more accessible to our diverse Queens, New York community. Our comprehensive, patient-centered care applies to a variety of cancers, and treatment comes in many different forms, including surgery, immunotherapy, clinical trials, and so much more.

Hospice Care
The Ferrara Family Center for Hospice Care at JHMC provides a variety of compassionate end-of-life care services to support patients and their families, with spacious patient rooms and comfortable family meeting and lounge areas, grief and bereavement counseling, family advocacy, and holistic care for physical pain and emotional, social, and spiritual needs.

Welcome to Jamaica Hospital

Since 1891, Jamaica Hospital has stood as a pillar of healthcare in the culturally diverse community of Queens, New York. We are dedicated to providing compassionate care in a welcoming atmosphere.

As a community hospital in the heart of Queens, we provide primary and specialty care services when you need it most. We offer a wide range of high-quality services, supporting patients at every stage of life.

Whether you need emergency care, specialized treatments, or routine health services, Jamaica Hospital is here for you. Discover how we care for our community and why we have been a trusted hospital in Queens, New York, for well over a century.
