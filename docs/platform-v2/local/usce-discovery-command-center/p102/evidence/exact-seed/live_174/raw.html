Four Emory researchers elected to Association of American Physicians

The Association of American Physicians recently elected four Emory researchers to its membership: Allan Levey, K. M. Venkat Narayan, Guido Silvestri and Jindan Yu. They were selected for their visionary advancements in basic or translational biomedical research and join 2,700 active members across the United States, Canada and the world.
