The Office of Graduate Medical Education at UC Health , in collaboration with the University of Cincinnati College of Medicine (UCCOM), hosts over 100 residency and fellowship programs across a diverse range of specialties, including medicine, dentistry, podiatry, and medical physics. With more than 70 of our programs accredited by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME), we are dedicated to providing world-class training and education to our residents and fellows. Our mission is to champion the development of patient-centered leaders through innovative, collaborative, and advocacy-driven approaches. We place a strong emphasis on learner support and development, wellness and wellbeing, and continuous innovation in medical education. Our guiding principles reflect our dedication to fostering a positive and growth-oriented environment: Commitment to Excellence Learner-Centered Approach Interprofessional Collaboration Wellness and Wellbeing Ethical Practice Patient-Centered Care Lifelong Learning and Growth Mindset Innovation and Research Community Engagement The development of learners into adaptive experts is a fundamental driver of our mission. We challenge ourselves to meet our residents' and fellows' expectations for high-quality learning experiences by providing the necessary foundation to maximize career potential and fulfill personal and professional goals in a supportive environment. We invite you to explore our programs and join us in our commitment to excellence in graduate medical education. Together, we can achieve our vision of becoming the destination training site for graduate medical education in the United States. Welcome to UCCOM and UC Health GME, where your journey to becoming a patient-centered leader begins! Thank you for your interest in our programs.
