Event Calendar Visiting Students Domestic Visiting Students Students who have completed their core clinical training and will be in the fourth year of medical education at their LCME- or AOA- accredited home institution in North America may apply for an elective experience at the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine as a Domestic Visiting Student Applicant. Visiting International Students The University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine (UPSOM) welcomes international students and invites applications for a visiting elective. UPSOM provides opportunities for a limited number of visiting international applicants worldwide.
