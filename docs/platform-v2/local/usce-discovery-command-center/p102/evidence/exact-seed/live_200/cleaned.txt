The College of Medicine provides the educational environment and opportunities to meet the needs of all medical residents and facilitate their professional, ethical, and personal development. This is consistent with the college’s mission to provide “a broad array of programs targeted at the education and training of physicians at the undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate levels.” The Board of Trustees, the College of Medicine Administration, and GME community are committed to and responsible for promoting patient safety and resident well-being, and for providing a supportive educational environment. The leadership of the College of Medicine Graduate Medical Education Program supports safe and appropriate patient care through appropriate curricula, evaluation, and resident supervision.
