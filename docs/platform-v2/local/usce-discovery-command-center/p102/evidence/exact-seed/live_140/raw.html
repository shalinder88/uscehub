CountyCare is a Health Plan Built Around You

CountyCare is a highly rated Medicaid managed care plan in Illinois, among only 22 percent of all Medicaid plans that received a four-star rating or higher this year*. With CountyCare, premium benefits like vision, dental and urgent care are included! Plus, you can earn Visa cash rewards for taking care of your health.

Available to everyone enrolled in HealthChoice Illinois, CountyCare is Cook County’s largest Medicaid health plan with access to more than 6,600+ primary care providers, 26,000+ specialists, 70+ hospital locations and 150+ urgent care sites throughout Cook County.

CountyCare is open to all Cook County residents enrolled through HealthChoice Illinois. If you are not signed up for Medicaid or if you are unsure, call CountyCare at (312) 864-8203 for help.

* The National Committee for Quality Assurance awarded CountyCare a four-star rating in 2025, rating it the highest-quality Medicaid plan in Illinois.
