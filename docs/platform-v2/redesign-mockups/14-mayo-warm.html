What "verified" means here

USCEHub is a directory, not a placement service. We confirm three specific things, in plain English. Anything we cannot confirm, we say so.

The institution's source URL works. The listing has been reviewed by an admin before publishing. Reviews are kept structurally separate from source-link verification, because reviews are opinions and verification is an audit. We don't let one substitute for the other.
