How USCEHub verifies programs

Four checks, each in plain English. A listing that fails any of them is flagged — never silently hidden.

NPI

NPI-Verified Posters

Institutions verify their NPI credentials before posting. Each poster's NPI number is checked against the national registry.

✓

Admin-Reviewed

Every listing is reviewed by our team for accuracy before publishing. We don't auto-publish poster submissions.

★

Community Reviews

Moderated community feedback, separate from source verification. Reviews are opinions; verification is an audit.

⌥

Moderated Platform

Active moderation for quality assurance. Broken links are flagged "Needs recheck" within one cron cycle.

How USCEHub works

A directory, not a placement service. We do not apply for you. We make sure the link works.

STEP 01 · SEARCH

Filter by what matters

Specialty, state, fee range, visa support, time of year. Results prioritize source-linked, recently reviewed listings.

STEP 02 · OPEN SOURCE

Use the institution's link

Every listing carries the institution's own application URL. Apply at the source. Save listings to revisit, compare up to three side by side.

STEP 03 · BUDGET

Estimate the trip

Use the cost estimator for housing, food, transport, and insurance. USMLE / ECFMG / visa / airfare fees are not included.
